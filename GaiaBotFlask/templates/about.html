About Us

At GaiaHarvesting, we have produced the GaiaBot as a solution to the ongoing labour shortages in the agricultural industry. The GaiaBot is a fully autonomous robot that is capable of harvesting crops and depositing them in a basket. The GaiaBot is equipped with a camera and sensors to detect the location of the crops and to avoid obstacles. The GaiaBot is also capable of returning to its charging port when its battery is low. The GaiaBot is designed to be user-friendly and easy to operate.
